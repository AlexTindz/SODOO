Dedicated Service for Mawogola North Constituency

About The Leader

Born in Sembabule District, Hon. Aine Godfrey Sodo Kaguta has dedicated his life to public service. With a Bachelor's degree in Public Administration from Makerere University and over 15 years of community leadership, he brings both expertise and passion to Mawogola North.

Core Principles

  • Transparent Governance
  • Inclusive Development
  • Sustainable Agriculture
  • Quality Education

Political Journey

From local council leadership to his current role as MP, Hon. Kaguta has consistently championed grassroots development, earning recognition as "Most Accessible Leader" by the Uganda Parliamentary Forum in 2022.
